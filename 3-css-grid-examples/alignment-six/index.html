Grid 6: align/justify-items vs align/justify-content

aligning or justifying "content" moves the whole box across the grid.

aligning or justifying "items" moves the items inside the box that is in the grid.

For example, we can move the entire box of content to the bottom left of the grid. then when all the items are in the bottom left, we can align the ITEMS to either the top or the bottom, or center OF that confined area.
